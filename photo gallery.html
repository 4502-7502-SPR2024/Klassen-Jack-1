Mizzou vs Kansas State: My experience

On September 16, 2023 Mizzou Football was hosting number 15 Kansas State. The game was tied at 27 a piece with 20 seconds left. Mizzou quaterback Brady Cook found his running back Cody Shrader for a 24 yard gain to put Mizzou on the brink of field goal range. Kicker Harrison Mevis ran out and drilled a 60 yard field goal as time expired to win the game in walk-off fashion. The following photos highlight my experience before, during, and after the game.
