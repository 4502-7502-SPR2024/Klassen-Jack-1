Best BBQ spots to try in Kansas City

Kansas City is widely known for having some of the best BBQ food in the entire country. You must go to the following seven BBQ restaurants to get a taste of what Kansas City BBQ is all about.

Joe's Kansas City Bar -B- Que

Our original joint was established in 1996, in the gas station at the corner of 47th and Mission in Kansas City, Kansas.

3002 West 47th Avenue, Kansas City, KS 66103
(913) 722-3366

Q39

Come on into the welcoming neighborhood, where are chef-driven ideas first came to life.

1000 W 39th St, Kansas City, MO 64111
(816) 255 3753

Hawg Jaw Que & Brew

Hawg Jaw Que & Brew is one of Kansas City’s best BBQ restaurants that has been around for nearly 20 years.

1900 Swift Ave, Kansas City, MO 64116
(816) 741 4294

Burnt End BBQ

Burnt End BBQ located in Crown Center is a top-tier BBQ restaurant that offers a wide range of different types of BBQ food at a cheap price.

2450 Grand Blvd #130, Kansas City, MO 64108
(816) 601 0444

LC'S Bar-B-Q

LC’s Bar-B-Q is an award-winning BBQ restaurant in Kansas City that was established in 1986.

5800 Blue Pkwy, Kansas City, MO 63131
(816) 923 4484

Slaps BBQ

Slap’s BBQ opened in June of 2014. We sell out of our meat every day which means that if you want a chance to try our great BBQ you will have to show up quite early.

553 Central Avenue, Kansas City, KS 66101
(913) 213 3736

Arthur Bryant's BBQ

Welcome to Arthur Bryant’s BBQ featuring meat that’s slow-smoked with a combination of hickory and oak woods then splashed with our famous Arthur Bryant’s sauce.

1727 Brooklyn Avenue, Kansas City, MO 64127
(816) 231 1123
